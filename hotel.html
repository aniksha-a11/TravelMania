Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a hotel with Pathway?
Enter your city, check-in, and check-out dates in the search form, browse available hotels, and click "Book Now" to complete your reservation.
What is the cancellation policy?
Cancellation policies vary by hotel and rate type. Check the hotel’s booking details or contact our support team for assistance.
Are the prices shown per night or total?
Prices are typically shown per night, excluding taxes and fees, unless otherwise specified. The total cost will be displayed during booking.
Can I book for a group or family?
Yes, you can book for groups or families. Specify the number of guests during your search, or contact support for tailored options.
How do I contact customer support?
Reach out via our website’s contact form or email support@pathway.com for prompt assistance.
